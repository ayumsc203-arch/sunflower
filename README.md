# Sunflower AR Effect

An interactive Augmented Reality (AR) application that tracks your hand gestures via webcam to grow a virtual sunflower plant on your screen.

## How to use

1. Run the script: `python main.py`
2. **Left Hand Pinch-Out**: Spread your index finger and thumb on your left hand to smoothly grow the sunflower stem and branches upwards from the bottom.
3. **Right Hand Pinch-Out**: Spread your index finger and thumb on your right hand to make the yellow sunflowers bloom at the tips of the branches.
4. **Quit**: Press the `q` key on your keyboard to close the application.

*Note: Ensure your webcam is connected and the room is well-lit for accurate hand tracking.*
